    Tracks Motifs in a moving window, such that the top priority item can always be obtained efficiently.

    Tracks Motifs in a moving window, such that the top priority item can always be obtained efficiently. Items can be removed on the left and insert on the right.

    Invariants: head of cache is top ranked (minimal rank), and also leftmost position. Priority decreases (i.e. rank increases) monotonically going left to right. Motifs are sorted by position. The minimizer of the current k-length window is always the first motif in the list.
